Daniya's top favourite dishes!

Let's see how I rank them.

1. Burmese Khowsuey

Khow suey, is a noodle soup made of egg noodles and curried beef or chicken with coconut milk, served with a variety of contrasting condiments.

2. Pani puri

Panipuri is a deep-fried hollow shell, filled with a combination of potatoes, chickpeas and spices. It is often flavoured with chili powder.

3. Chilli crisp noodles

I found a recipe online and loved it. It is basically noodles smothered in butter and chilli crisp oil.
